How it works

1

Upload

Police/Authorities upload a sketch, CCTV image, photo, or any available image of the person whose identity needs to be resolved.

→
2

AI Matching

The system compares the uploaded face against the Aadhaar database, or any other Govt. identification proof. It can also be matched against criminal records if valid.

→
3

Ranked results

Top matches are ranked by similarity percentage. Full identity details are retrieved for each shortlisted person.

→
4

Resolution

Police or the authorities review the shortlist and confirm the correct identity using supporting information.

Where Shodh can be used

🔎

Criminal investigation

Match a suspect sketch drawn from a victim's or witness's memory against the criminal database when no digital evidence exists. Since every Indian citizen holds an Aadhaar card, or other Govt. ID; Identification is possible even when the person has no prior criminal record.

→
🆘

Disaster management

Identify survivors or victims of floods, earthquakes, or accidents when they carry no documents.

→
🏥

Medical emergency

Identify an unconscious patient and retrieve their medical history and emergency contacts instantly in case of emergency.

→
👶

Missing persons

Find lost children, elderly patients, or missing individuals by matching their photo or sketch to a database.

👤

⚠️ This is a shortlisted match only. Verification by authorized personnel is required before any action is taken.
